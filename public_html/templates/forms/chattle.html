1. LOAN
Nexgen Equipment Rental will make the Loan to the Grantor during the Availability Period of a principal amount not at any time exceeding the Loan Amount, subject to the fulfilment or waiver of provisions of this Agreement and in reliance upon the representations and warranties made in clause 10.
2. PURPOSE
The Grantor must apply the proceeds of the Loan for the sole purpose of the purchase of the Goods.
3. DRAWDOWN
The Grantor must request the Loan by giving a Drawdown Notice to Nexgen Equipment Rental to be received not later than 11 a.m. (Sydney time) three Business Days, or any shorter period agreed by Nexgen Equipment Rental, prior to the proposed Drawdown Date. The Drawdown Notice must be signed by an Authorised Officer on behalf of the Grantor, if a corporate party, specify the Drawdown Date, which must be a Business Day, and specify in dollars the principal amount of the Loan to be drawn. The Drawdown Notice is effective on receipt by Nexgen Equipment Rental and when given is irrevocable.
4. INTEREST
The Grantor must pay interest on the outstanding Loan. Each Repayment Instalment Amount includes a component of interest. Interest accrues on the outstanding principal amount of the Loan from day to day and for the actual number of days elapsed and must be computed on the basis of a year of 365 days. The Grantor will pay all accrued interest in arrears on each Repayment Instalment Date and on the date of repayment or prepayment of the Loan or of the applicable part of the Loan, in the event of partial repayment or prepayment.
5. REPAYMENT
The Grantor must repay to Nexgen Equipment Rental in full the principal amount of the Loan on the Final Repayment Date, together with all other Secured Moneys. The Grantor must repay the Loan by instalments prior to final repayment by repaying to Nexgen Equipment Rental the Repayment Instalment Amount of the Loan on each Repayment Instalment Date.
6. PREPAYMENT
The Grantor may prepay all or any part of any outstanding Loan, following prior notice to Nexgen Equipment Rental of not less than three Business Days. A prepayment notice is irrevocable and the Grantor must prepay in compliance with that notice. Prepayment of a Loan must be made together with all accrued interest but without premium or 9.2 penalty. Any amount prepaid may not be redrawn under this Agreement. If partial prepayment of the Loan is made, the Loan Amount is reduced by the amount prepaid. If full prepayment of all outstanding Loans is made, the Loan Amount is reduced to zero.
7.PAYMENTS
The Grantor must make any payment to Nexgen Equipment Rental required under this Agreement in dollars, not later than 11 a.m. (Sydney time) on the due date for payment by bank cheque or in other immediately available funds to the account or place notified at any time by Nexgen Equipment Rental to the Grantor. Any payment by the Grantor required under this Agreement falling due on a day which is not a Business Day must be made on the immediately preceding Business Day in the same calendar month with appropriate adjustment for interest. The Grantor must make any payment required under this Agreement without any set-off, counterclaim or other deduction or withholding whether on account of taxes or otherwise, except for any tax deduction or withholding compelled by law. The Grantor must indemnify Nexgen Equipment Rental upon demand for any tax deduction or withholding required or made from any payment due under this Agreement.
8.CHARGE
The Grantor charges by way of specific equitable mortgage the Collateral to Nexgen Equipment Rental as a continuing security for payment by the Grantor of the Secured Moneys. This Agreement shall rank first in point of priority in relation to the Collateral as against every other security interest created at any time in relation to the Collateral, except for any Permitted Security.
